The Concept

The West Rand has the appetite. It never had the atlas.

Ruimsig, Featherbrooke, Little Falls and Wilgeheuwel are full of households who eat out every weekend and still end up choosing between the same few chains. The Gathering Atlas replaces that choice with four fully realised cultural zones under a single roof — so nobody at the table compromises, and nobody has to drive to Sandton for a proper night out.
